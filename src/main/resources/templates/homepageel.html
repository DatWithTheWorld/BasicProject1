Introduction to Some Industry Groups

Below Are Some Industry Groups And Characteristics Of That Group

Science Education and Training Industry Group

The field of Education Science and Teacher Training focuses on research in education and teacher training. It concentrates on how individuals learn and develop, effective teaching and assessment methods, as well as the preparation and training of professional teachers...

Computer and IT Industry Group

Computer and Information Technology industry group focuses on the research, development, and application of technologies related to computers, software, computer networks, and information systems...

Artistic Gifted Industry Group

Includes fields such as stage art, music, painting, sculpture, literature, and film...

Humanities Sciences Industry Group

The field of Humanities Sciences focuses on researching and understanding various aspects of human beings, society, and culture. Students in this field study subjects such as history, philosophy, language, culture, psychology, and social research methods.

Social And Behavioral Sciences Industry Group

This field includes various disciplines such as psychology, sociology, economics, political science, anthropology, law, education, media studies, and many other fields.

Journalism - Communication Industry Group

This field encompasses various sectors such as journalism, publishing, television, radio, advertising, digital media, and information technology related to the transmission of information and interaction between individuals and media.

Business and Management Industry Group

This field includes various sectors such as economics, marketing, finance, strategic management, human resource management, production management, and project management.

Law Industry Group

This field includes various sectors such as civil law, criminal law, labor law, business law, international law, judicial law, and many other fields.
